Plastic ballpoint pens

Plastic ballpoint pens create plastic waste since they can’t be refilled and are thrown away after use. Use refillable pens instead. They last longer, reduce waste, and only need ink refills.

    Plastic ballpoint pens

  • Bic Cristal Plastic Pens
  • Plastic Waste : Thrown away after use, leads to pollution.
  • Can’t Be Refilled : New ones must be bought again and again.
  • Millions Discarded : So many are used worldwide that they harm the environment.

  • Less Plastic Waste The whole pen doesn't need to be thrown away, all we need is just change the ink.
  • Saves Money Buying ink refills is cheaper than buying new pens.
  • Lasts Longer Reusable pens are made stronger and can be used for years.
