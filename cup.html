Reusable Cups

Styrofoam coffee cups are bad because they can’t be recycled, and can harm animals, and take hundreds of years to break down.

Instead, use reusable coffee cups made of metal or bamboo. They can be washed and reused, keep drinks warm, and don’t create waste.

    Plastic Cup

  • It takes hundreds of years to break down
  • If animals eat it, it can harm or kill them, and it fills up landfills and pollutes oceans.
