Notes

Post-it notes add to paper waste and are hard to recycle because of the sticky part. Choose digital notes or whiteboards. These save paper, are reusable, and keep your notes organized.

    Post-it Notes

  • Hard to Recycle : Sticky glue makes recycling difficult
  • Creates Paper Waste : Use once and thrown away.
  • Short Use Life : Often falls off or gets lost quickly.

It's easy to note and easy to read back. And no more waste!
